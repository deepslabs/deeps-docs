---
title: DHC Farming
description: How to participate in DHC Farming as an Owner or Voter
---

For [DHC Farming](https://dhcs.deeps.fi/beta_testnet), there are two types of roles: **Owner** and **Voter**.

* **Owner** is the person running the DHC, which can be the project owner, KOL, community leader, etc.
* **Voter** is the person who delegates their DPS to the node for farming DPS staking rewards.

Users can choose to be an Owner, a Voter, or both.
